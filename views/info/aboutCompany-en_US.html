History

  • The Company history begins at 1991, when ArgusSoft Company was created under Professor E.G. Oykhman guidance.
  • The main directions of ArgusSoft activity were promotion of object-oriented (OO) technology and development of custom application software for customers in Russia and CIS countries. The company was the first in the country to become an official distributor of Rational Rose software products, including the system of visual modeling Rational Rose.
  • By 1996 Argussoft Company has implemented its own Methodology and Technology for design and development of software based on the Rational Unified Process (RUP) and Rapid Application Development (RAD), which was patented in the United States and continues to be improved in accordance with the latest achievements in the field of OO technology.
  • In these years the specialists of the Company have successfully completed over one hundred development projects for organizations in Russia and USA including Central Bank of Russia, Central Election Committee of Russia, the National Bank of Republic of Tatarstan, KazakhTelecom, Renaissance Software Inc., Neopost-Online, Dymo, Caterpillar and others.
