Reset Your BasicMed Account Password

How to reset your BasicMed Course Password?

  1. Enter the email address associated with your account.
  2. Click the “Email Me a Code to Change Password” button above.
  3. Check your inbox for an email with the subject “AOPA BasicMed Course Password Reset.” Note this email may take up to 15 mins to be received.
  4. In the email, note the code or select and copy the six-digit code.
  5. Return to this password reset webpage and enter or paste in the six-digit code. Then click submit.
  6. Enter your desired new password in both boxes. They must match.
  7. Click the “Save” button to reset the password.
  8. Return to the course and log in with your new password.

*If you have not received the email, check your junk/spam folder for an email from donotreply@aopa.org. Still do not see it, add this email address to your email contacts and repeat the steps above to have a new email sent.
